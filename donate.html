How Your Donation Helps Communities in Haiti

Every contribution to our Haiti charity supports sustainable programs that create lasting change. Here's how your support makes a difference:

Clean Water Access

Fund wells and water filtration systems that provide safe drinking water to villages without reliable access. Clean water reduces disease and improves daily life.

Educational Programs

Supply school materials, support teachers, and help children access the education they deserve. Education is the foundation for Haiti's future.

Disaster Relief

Provide emergency aid and long-term rebuilding support when earthquakes and hurricanes strike. Haiti faces ongoing recovery from natural disasters.

Community Empowerment

Invest in sustainable development projects that help communities become self-sufficient and build a stronger future for generations to come.

Frequently Asked Questions About Donating to Haiti

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes. Haitian Sensation Mission is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (EIN: 82-3623088). Your donation is tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt for your records after completing your donation.

How is my donation used?

Donations fund clean water projects, educational programs, disaster relief efforts, and community development initiatives in Haiti. Through our partnership with Zeffy, 100% of your donation goes directly to these programs—no administrative fees are deducted.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes. We process all donations through Zeffy, a secure payment platform built specifically for nonprofits. Your payment information is encrypted and protected, and is never stored on our servers.

Can I set up a recurring monthly donation?

Yes. When you click "Donate," you can choose to make a one-time gift or set up automatic monthly contributions. Recurring donations provide ongoing support that helps us plan and sustain our programs in Haiti.
